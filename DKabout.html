Who am i, and why i chose this topic?

My name is Amy and i am a student at NCI, currently studying Bachelors in computing. I really love music, computers and science!

The reason i chose to talk about the life on land in the Amazon is to try and spread awareness of just how bad it has gotten there. I first learnt about the issues in the Amazon back in secondary school during geography and since then i have been trying to spread awareness. The Amazon is an essential part of our lives as humans since it is home to at least 10% of the world's known species across all types of life! I hope that from my research that i have managed to educate some people and for them to spread the word.
